How to Setup Seama

  1. Invite Seama to Your Server: Click the "Invite Seama" button and select your server.
  2. Grant Permissions: Ensure Seama has the necessary permissions (e.g., Manage Messages, Ban Members).
  3. Configure via Commands: Use !setup to initialize Seama in your server.
  4. Customize Settings: Access the dashboard to fine-tune moderation settings and custom commands.
  5. Join Support Server: Get help and updates in our support server.
